# **X (Twitter) App – Context Plan**

## **App Description**

X is a social media platform built around real-time public conversation using short-form posts. Users share thoughts, news, and media in a fast-paced feed designed for immediacy and discoverability. The platform allows following accounts, interacting with content, and participating in trending discussions.

---

## **Main Functions**

### **Posting**

- **Character Limit:** 280 characters (longer for premium users)
- **Media Attachments:** Up to 4 photos, videos, GIFs, polls
- **Post Creation:** Accessible via a prominent compose button
- **Draft Saving:** Users can save posts to drafts
- **Scheduling:** Posts can be scheduled (premium feature)
- **Visibility Options:** Public posts or followers-only

### **Commenting (Replying)**

- **Reply Threading:** Users can reply to any post, creating conversation threads
- **Reply Restrictions:** Original poster may limit replies (everyone, followers, mentioned users)
- **Nested Structure:** Replies appear under the original post chronologically or algorithmically
- **Quote Functionality:** Users can quote-post (repost with added commentary)

### **Threading**

- **Self-Replies:** Used to create multi-post threads
- **Thread Composer:** Write multiple posts as a single thread before publishing
- **“Show This Thread” Link:** Appears when viewing a post within a thread
- **Numbered Indicators:** e.g., “1/5” to show thread order

### **Additional Core Functions**

- **Reposting:** Share posts with or without comments
- **Liking:** Heart icon
- **Bookmarking:** Save posts privately
- **Sharing:** Via DM or external apps
- **Direct Messaging:** Private user conversations

---

## **Page Descriptions**

### **For You Page**

- **Algorithmic Feed:** Curated via interests, engagement, and trends
- **Content Mix:** Includes posts from accounts you don't follow
- **Personalization:** Optimized for predicted engagement
- **Ads Integration:** Promoted posts appear in the feed
- **Refresh Mechanism:** Pull-to-refresh or auto-refresh
- **Interaction Bar:** Like, reply, repost, share, bookmark
- **Trending Integration:** Surfaces content tied to current trends

---

### **Following Page**

- **Chronological Feed:** Reverse-chronological posts from followed accounts
- **Pure Following:** Only shows content from accounts you follow (plus their reposts)
- **Cleaner Experience:** Minimal algorithmic influence
- **Real-time Updates:** “Show new posts” banner
- **Full Interaction Options**

---

### **Profile Page**

#### **Header Section**

- Banner image
- Profile picture
- Display name + @username
- Join date
- Bio (160 characters)
- Location + website
- Following/followers count
- Follow/Unfollow (other profiles)
- Edit Profile (self)

#### **Tabs**

- **Posts:** Original posts + reposts
- **Replies:** Replies to others
- **Media:** Photos and videos
- **Likes:** Posts the user has liked

#### **Additional Elements**

- **Pinned Post:** One post can be pinned
- **Verification Badge:** Blue checkmark (subscription-based)

---

### **Notifications Page**

#### **Tabs**

- **All:** Every notification
- **Verified:** Only from verified accounts (premium)
- **Mentions:** Tagged @username mentions

#### **Notification Types**

- New followers
- Likes
- Reposts
- Replies
- Mentions
- Quote posts
- Poll results
- Spaces invitations

#### **Features**

- **Real-time Updates:** Unread badge counter
- **Quick Actions:** Like, reply, follow back directly
- **Filtering:** Mute or filter notification types
